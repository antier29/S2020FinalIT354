You want to start off by priming you eyes. I like to use a concealer as it covers any pigmentation my eyes might have. Next take either a pencil brush or flat brush and pack a blue shadow in your crease to about halfway of your eye. Next do the rest of the crease with a purple color. Take a clean brush and blend the two colors into the crease to difuse any sharp lines. Be sure not to blend too high as we want this color to say concentrated in one area.

You then want to take a small blending brush and take the blue shade and blend it onto the lid of the eye but only the first 1/3 of the eye. Take another blending brush and do the same with the purple but on the outer 1/3 of the eye.
To do the halo effect take a flat shader brush and place concealer onto the middle third of the eyelid where we don't have any shadow. You then can add a white shimmery shadow to that area. Or you can top off with some glitter for an extra bang.
Add some false lashes and tighline your eyes with either a black eyeliner or a blue/purple one in the bottom waterline.

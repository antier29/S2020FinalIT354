Trust the process! When doing a full face of makeup you might look crazy at first but it will all come together.
Start by filling in your brows. You will need to set them in place with a brow gel.
Next you need to prime your eyes with concealer and set with powder. Take a fluffy blending brush and add a warm red-brown shade to your crease. Really smoke this out as it is our base shade. Next take a smaller blending brush and use a darker red-brown shade in the lower crease. You want to place this color just below the previous one without going to high.

Next take a flat shader brush and some light concealer. If you have a shade that is too light for you, use it! Start by placing a dot on your eyelid and look up. The concealer will mark a part higher up on your eye. Use this as a guide for how high up you should place it. Set with a white eyeshadow. We want the color to pop! Take you finger and place a pinky white glitter shadow right over where we cut the crease.

Take a dark brown shade and place in the outer 3rd of your eye. Use a liquid eyeliner to make a tiny wing. We do not want this to draw attention away from the eyeshadow. Prime your face and put on foundation. Use a blender instead of a brush to give a flawless finish. Make sure the sponge is damp though! Conceal undereyes and highlight in the areas shown. Blend blend blend! Set the face with a light layer of powder.

Use an angled cheek brush when applying bronzer and contour, it makes it easier. Apply blush to the apples of the cheeks. Use a small brush to apply highlighter to the highpoints of your face.

Set the face and add mascara and fake eyelashes. Line your lips and put on some gloss. Now you are done! You are now ready!
